How to play this Game?

There are a number of active objects in this room that change their state when the cursor is hovered over. Each of the objects containes a simple (and probably tricky) puzzle. Crack these puzzles in sequence, get hints and find the key to open the door and finally exit the room!

You can use the mouse as well as keybord (WASD & Enter) to play this game.

Turn sound off if you prefer to play in silent mode.
